## Welcome to Local Content

This is a regular Markdown file located in `src/content/blog/`. It proves that you can now write content directly in your IDE alongside the posts managed in Notion.

Everything you expect from a standard Astro blog post should work, including:

- Layouts
- Styles
- Components (if this were an `.mdx` file)

This content will be seamlessly integrated into your post listings, tag pages, and RSS feeds.
